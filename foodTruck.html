Food Truck Concept


Neat

This concept was an assignment in university to create a graphic design profile for a food truck. These are some examples of my concept. The name is a fun play on words.

The concept for the food truck is vegan northern swedish sandwiches, so the name is neat instead of meat, and the word eat is included in the name. Both the "n" in the name and the tree logo represents Norrland.
Neat - Norrland eat!
