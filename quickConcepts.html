TimebloX

This idea came to me while i was hard at work studying. I felt like no focus app gave me the satisfaction of actually studying. I tried to think of what simple way i could track my focus, so i used a graph paper and filled a square for every 10 minute studied and i really liked the idea!
However, i did not like to have to manually fill a square every 10 minutes, and i knew i was going to forget to do it once in a while, thus creating the opposite effect and creating more work instead. I designed a simple quick wireframe, and came up with a name that conveyed both the function(tracking time) and the unique concept(rewarding a block), TimebloX.

A big inspiration for this was GitHub and how they display the commits on a user's profile, displayed as green blocks.
